Hawaiian Chicken Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 6 oz fried chicken
  • One lettuce leaf
  • 1/2 TBSP BBQ
  • Buns
  • Pineapple

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Grill pineaplle on stove on a cast iron pan.
  3. Cook chicken for 19 minutes.
  4. Assemble using all ingredients to make the best chicken sandwich ever!
