The Perfect Burger

Description

This is a really really really really tasty burger with prime Wisconsin highland cow beef!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz beef
  • 1/2 TBSP ketchup / BBQ / honey mustard
  • Buns
  • Pickles
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2 Tomato slices

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Cut vegetable toppings.
  3. Put Burger in oven for 12 minutes.
  4. Assemble all ingredients and enjoy a tasty meal
